Business Leadership: Management Fundamentals

Grade 12, University/College Preparation — Explore leadership skills, management functions, ethics, and how to effectively lead people and organizations in the business world.

Unit 1: Foundations of Management Unit 2: Leading Unit 3: Management Challenges Unit 4: Planning & Controlling Unit 5: Organizing

Course Overview

What is leadership? How do people effectively manage others? In this Business Leadership (BOH4M) course, you will explore the development of leadership skills and how these skills are used to effectively manage people and companies. You will also understand how managers function within organizations and solve real world problems. The roles, responsibilities, skills, and functions of management will also be explored. As you learn about the factors that contribute to effective leadership practices, emphasis will be placed on the importance of ethics and social responsibility. Learning how to communicate effectively for business purposes is also embedded throughout the course. By the end of your learning experience, you should be able to clearly understand what it takes to work successfully in positions of leadership and management.

Wherever you work, whether in a large corporation, your own private company, in the public sector, or a non-profit organization, you will need to be aware of the functions of management, leadership skills, organizational plans, and how to tackle challenges. To accomplish this, we will try to use as many real world examples and cases as a tool for learning in this Business Leadership course.
